Cliff Mark – Professional Real Estate Inspector

Cliff is the owner of Careful Home Inspections. He built over 350 homes in his career as a home builder and has inspected over 3,000 homes in his 12 years as a home inspector. He is very knowledgeable about how a home is supposed to be built but he also knows that no home is perfect.

His experienced judgment allows him to know when your prospective home’s imperfections are critical and when they need to be understood in the context of its unique and specific characteristics. You get his wealth of construction knowledge and experienced judgement with every Careful Home Inspection.
